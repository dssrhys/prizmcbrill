PRIVACY POLICY

We value you your privacy

Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Prizmic & Brill will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described on the right. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.

Resale or disclosure of information to third parties

Prizmic & Brill does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.

Browser information collected on the website

Prizmic & Brill analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.

Privacy of our email list

Prizmic & Brill maintains an email list to keep Prizmic & Brill' members informed about breaking events. Individuals are invited to join our mailing list by signing up online or contacting Prizmic & Brill directly.
We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized Prizmic & Brill staff, including other list subscribers.
