Bauer International (USA)

Bauer International Inc. has established an unparalleled reputation as one of the finest providers of hand crafted furnishings, accessories and fixtures to the hospitality industry, golf and country clubs, retailers, designers, and specialty events. Bauer International offers a one-stop shop of luxury fixtures and items, sych as retail space design, accessory, and fixture selection, importing, manufacturing and implementation. The company maintains a highly qualified staff of designers and manufacturers, who are able to transform an empty room into a time capsule of turn of the century luxury.
